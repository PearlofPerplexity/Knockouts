Our Story

Katie is a professionally-trained cellist who received her Bachelor of Arts in Music at University of Wisconsin Eau Claire.

She has has over a decade of experience as a cello teacher as well as extensive experience in solo, ensemble and orchestra settings alike.

Most notably, She performed at Carnegie Hall in New York City in the Honor's Performance Series High School Honors Orchestra.

Kyle is a classically-trained guitarist and percussionist with over twenty years of experience.

He is a singer-songwriter that has performed at wedding ceremonies, church services, recitals as well as busking.

When he married Katie in 2018, they blended their musical backgrounds together to create their duo: The Knockouts.
